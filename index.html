Our Story

We Started This Because Bad Advice Cost Us Money

SP

The Smart Picks Team

Independent Review Blog · Est. 2024

A few years back, I bought a laptop based on a glowing YouTube review — turned out the reviewer had never even opened the box. I returned it three days later, out ₹2,000 in restocking fees and no closer to a real answer. That frustration is exactly why Smart Picks Hub exists.

In 2024, I started writing the kind of reviews I wished I'd had: honest, detailed, written after actually using the product. Within six months, friends and family were forwarding the links to people they'd never met. That's when I knew this was something real.

Today we cover everything from budget tech to home essentials — always with the same rule: if we wouldn't recommend it to someone we care about, we don't recommend it here.
